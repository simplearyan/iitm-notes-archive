Calculating minors and cofactors of a matrix:
  • If AAA is a square matrix, then the minor of the entry in the iii-th row and jjj-th column (denoted by MijM_{ij}Mij​) is the determinant of the submatrix formed by deleting the iii-th row and jjj-th column.

   A−1=1det(A)adj(A)A^{-1}=\frac{1}{det(A)}adj(A)A−1=det(A)1​adj(A).

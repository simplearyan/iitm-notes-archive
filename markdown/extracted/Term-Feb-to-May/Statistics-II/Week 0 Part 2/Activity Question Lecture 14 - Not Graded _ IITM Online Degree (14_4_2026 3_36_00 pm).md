 **Concepts covered:

**For two events $A$ and $B$:

$A∪B$: Either $A$ occurs or $B$ occurs or both occurs.

$A⋂B$: $A$ and $B$ occurs.

 De Morgan’s laws:

$(A ∪ B)^c$ = $A^c ⋂ B^c and (A ⋂ B)^c = A^c ∪ B^c$
